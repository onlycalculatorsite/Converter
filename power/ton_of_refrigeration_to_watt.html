Understanding Tons of Refrigeration and Watts

What is a Ton of Refrigeration?

A Ton of Refrigeration (TR) is a unit of power used to describe the heat-extraction capacity of refrigeration and air conditioning equipment. It is equivalent to the rate of heat transfer required to melt one short ton (2,000 lbs) of pure ice at 0 ¬∞C in 24 hours.

What is a Watt?

A Watt (W) is the SI unit of power, equivalent to one joule per second. It is used to quantify the rate of energy transfer.
