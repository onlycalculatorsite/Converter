Understanding KPA and PSI

What is Kilopascal (KPA)?

The Kilopascal (kPa) is a unit of pressure in the metric system. One kilopascal is equal to 1,000 pascals. It is widely used in scientific and engineering fields to measure pressure.

What is PSI?

Pounds per square inch (PSI) is a unit of pressure in the imperial system of measurement. It is the pressure resulting from a force of one pound-force applied to an area of one square inch.

Why Convert KPA to PSI?
